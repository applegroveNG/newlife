Who We Are

Mission

Our mission is to positively influence today's society through the most powerful transformative tool, Jesus Christ. We strive to do this by initiating strategic programs tailored toward human capital development and social integration across Nigeria, Africa, and the global community.

Vision

Our vision is to empower a generation of leaders and change-makers by providing access to quality education, rooted in the values of faith, excellence, and service. We aim to bridge gaps in educational opportunities while fostering holistic development—spiritually, academically, and socially—for a brighter future across Nigeria and beyond.

The New Life Education Trust’s Scholarship Fund

The New Life Education Trust’s Scholarship Fund is dedicated to supporting Nigerian students across federal and state universities. We believe in empowering students to pursue their academic dreams without the burden of financial constraints. Our scholarships cover essential expenses, such as tuition fees, accommodation, feeding, and other necessary academic costs, ensuring students have the resources they need to thrive.

Eligibility Criteria & Application Parameters

  • Minimum CGPA of 3.5 on a scale of 5.0 (First year students do not need this requirement)
  • Must be in 200 Level or above at the University of Lagos
  • Provide proof of university admission (admission letter)
  • Submit a Letter of Identification, stamped by the HOD (Head of Department) of their respective department
  • Provide academic results showcasing current CGPA
